  2. Nickname for Napolean III. ↩

  3. Passenger boats which ply on the Seine. ↩

  4. Waterspout. ↩

  5. An Arab dish. ↩

  6. Like blue letter-cards but used as telegrams, they are sent through special tubes. ↩

  7. A play on the words “Sceaux” (buckets) and “Sots” (fools). —Translator’s Note ↩

  8. Désabusé⁠—Dissillusioned. Des abusés⁠—Amongst the deluded. —Translator’s note ↩
